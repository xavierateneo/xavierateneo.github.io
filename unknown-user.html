Account Not Found

Your Google account was authenticated successfully, but we couldn't find a matching Canvas account in our system.

What does this mean?

  • Your Google account is valid, but it's not linked to a Canvas account yet
  • You may be a new student or employee whose Canvas account hasn't been created
  • You might be using the wrong account type (Faculty vs. Student login)

What should you do?

  • New students: Your Canvas account will be created within 24-48 hours of enrollment
  • New employees: Contact XU CISO to activate your Canvas account
  • Current users: Make sure you're using the correct login option for your account type
